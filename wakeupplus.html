Privacy Policy

Wake Up Plus takes your privacy seriously. To better protect your privacy, we provide this privacy policy notice explaining the way your personal information is collected and used in the Wake Up Plus mobile alarm app.

Collection of Routine Information

Wake Up Plus collects basic information needed to operate the app and improve its functionality. This may include device type, operating system version, app version, alarm settings, notification preferences, and crash reports. We do not collect sensitive data such as location, contacts, or microphone access unless you explicitly enable a feature that requires it.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage information such as how often alarms are set, which features are used, and whether the app is opened regularly. This data helps us identify bugs, improve the alarm experience, and make Wake Up Plus more reliable. The information is aggregated and cannot personally identify you.

Third-Party Services

Wake Up Plus may use third-party services for crash reporting, analytics, and push notifications. These providers may collect limited technical information about your device and app use, and their data handling is governed by their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information to any third party.

Links to Third-Party Content

The app may include links to third-party websites or app stores for support, policies, or additional features. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those external services. Please review the privacy policy of any third-party service you use.

Security

The security of your information is important to us. While we use commercially reasonable measures to protect app data, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Please keep your device protected with a passcode and avoid sharing sensitive information on shared devices.

Changes To This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy is effective as of July 3, 2026 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future, which will be in effect immediately after being posted on this page.

We reserve the right to update or change our Privacy Policy at any time and you should check this Privacy Policy periodically. If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will notify you either through the email address you have provided us, or by placing a prominent notice in the app.
